Personalized Medicine for the 21st Century

Mission, Vision and Values

Our Mission

Translate genetic technology into greater patient empowerment and health.

Our Vision

Helix Health will deliver personalized medical services with utmost professionalism and discretion, educate physicians and the community about genetic technologies and their use in optimizing health, oppose all forms of genetic discrimination and advance the use of medically validated genetic technology.

Our Values

Helix Health will:

  • Offer genetic tests that have clinical validity and utility and are provided through CLIA-certified laboratories.
  • Provide pre- and post-test genetic counseling to ensure fully informed patient participation.
  • Responsibly broaden access to genetic testing for those consumers who are motivated to learn more about their genetic constitution with regard to disease risk.
