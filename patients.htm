Personalized Medicine and Your Health

With a specialist genetics team behind you, the full power of your genetic profile—from family history and specific genetic tests—is incorporated into your health care as completely as it can be.

The Helix Health team uses validated models and applicable genetic testing to provide each of our patients with a comprehensive risk assessment that addresses how family history impacts the risk for future disease as well as how a personal history of a medical condition impacts the risk for children or other family members. Our goal is to create a plan of screening, prevention, and treatment that is targeted to not only your genetic background, but also your preferences and objectives.

Helix Health provides patients ongoing consultation that can include comprehensive assessment and coordination of applicable genetic testing for:

  • Inherited cancer risk
  • Predisposition to chronic diseases such as stroke, diabetes and heart disease
  • Pharmacogenetics - prediction of response and reaction to medications
  • Preconception genetic evaluation - likelihood of transmitting inherited diseases to future children
