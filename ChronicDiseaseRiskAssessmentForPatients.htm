Chronic Disease Predisposition Risk Assessment

Understanding What Contributes to Your Risk

Chronic diseases—such as diabetes, heart disease, high blood pressure, and stroke—are a significant cause of death and disability in the United States. It is well established that both environmental factors (diet, physical activity, tobacco use, etc) and genetic factors (family history) contribute to these often devastating conditions. Despite this, the majority of widely used risk assessment tools do not take family history into account, focusing only on personal history and environmental risks.

Understanding your risk for chronic disease is the first step in effectively managing it. At Helix Health we begin with a detailed three to four generation family history and incorporate this information into our comprehensive risk assessment. Together with your primary care physician, we will work to develop a prevention and early detection plan targeted to your specific needs.

Personal and family histories change over time and research on genetics and chronic disease risk is rapidly evolving. Through our commitment to continuity of care via quarterly patient contact and medical literature reviews, Helix Health patients are kept up to date on relevant new advances.
