To refer a patient to Helix Health, please choose from one of the following options:

  • Download and complete our referral form and fax it to us at 646-707-3228
  • Call us at (888) 584-8999 (option 2) with your referral. Please indicate:
    • Referring provider's name and contact number
    • Patient's full name and date of birth
    • Patient's preferred contact phone number
    • The reason for the referral
